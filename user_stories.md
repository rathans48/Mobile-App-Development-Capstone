Story 1 (Signup): As a user, I want to register an account using my username, email, and password so I can access the app.
Story 2 (Login): As a user, I want to log in using my email and password to view my dashboard.
Story 3 (Home): As a user, I want to see a home screen with the app logo and a list of items fetched from a server.
Story 4 (Detail): As a user, I want to tap an item on the home screen to navigate to a detailed view of that item.
Story 5 (API): As a user, I want the app to display real data integrated from an external API.
Story 6 (Settings Menu): As a user, I want to open a settings menu via an icon to access my preferences.
Story 7 (Settings Screen): As a user, I want to view a dedicated settings screen to manage app configurations.
Story 8 (Local Storage): As a user, I want my preferences to be saved locally so they persist between app launches.
Story 9 (Notifications): As a user, I want to receive push notifications to alert me of important updates.
